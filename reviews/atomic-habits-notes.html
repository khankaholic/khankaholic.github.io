Book Review · Nov 15, 2025

Atomic Habits: What Actually Stuck

I used the framework from this book for roughly three months with two goals: daily writing and more consistent technical reading. The most useful part was not motivation. It was environment design.

What worked

  • I attached writing to a fixed cue: first coffee, then 20 minutes of drafting.
  • I lowered friction by keeping a single notes file open all day.
  • I tracked streaks weekly, not daily, to avoid perfectionism loops.

What did not

Habit trackers alone were insufficient. Without clear outcomes, I was checking boxes while avoiding difficult work. The adjustment was pairing every habit with a concrete output target.

Takeaway

Systems beat willpower, but only when the system points to meaningful deliverables. I now design habits around shipped artifacts, not activity counts.
